Rules and Policies for Buyers

To help make buying on AMP a safe and enjoyable experience, we've put in place a number of policies and guidelines.

Many of these are based on local legal requirements, but others are based on our experience of what will help ensure a fair marketplace for everyone. So, before you make a counter-offer (or bid) or buy on AMP, read our policies for buyers listed below.
